Customized Singular Polls

OPRA lets you tailor every poll to your needs — choose question types (Poll or Allocation), select voting algorithms, and control visibility. Anonymous voting is supported so participants can respond confidentially. Set a concise title and an optional description that voters can refer to throughout the process.

Organized & Analyzed Results

After a poll ends, OPRA automatically computes and analyzes results using powerful preference aggregation algorithms. Get clear, structured outcomes that help your group make better, data-driven decisions — no manual tallying required.
