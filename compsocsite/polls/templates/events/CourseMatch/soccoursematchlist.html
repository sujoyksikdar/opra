More Information

Assigning courses to students is hard! We got you covered. We will do our best to find solutions that are both fair and efficient based on your preferences by using the latest techniques in matching theory and resource allocation.
There are several complex, open questions about how best to make decisions on assigning scarce resources to interested agents. These questions are the subject of active research at the intersection of computer science, artificial intelligence, economics, psychology and philosophy with implications for politics and public policy as well.
If you would like to learn more, please contact Sujoy Sikdar, who is always happy to discuss resource allocation, matching, computational social science, game theory and related topics.
