Polls

Use Polls to collect ranked preferences from a group of users. Click the Polls tab in the left sidebar after logging in. You can create new polls, view polls you created, and see polls you've been invited to participate in. Use the search box to filter polls by name.

Creating a Poll

Click "Create New" and complete the four configuration steps.

Step 1 — Basic Info
  • 1Title — Give a unique name to your poll.
  • 2Description — Add meaningful context for voters.
  • 3Question type — Select Poll (pre-selected) for voting, or Allocation for resource allocation.
  • 4Allow Ties — Let voters assign equal preference to multiple options.
Step 2 — Alternatives
  • 1Enter an option name and optionally attach a photo.
  • 2Click "+" to add it. Repeat until all options are added.
  • 3Click "×" to remove an option.
Step 3 — Invite Users
  • 1Search for registered OPRA users by username and add them.
  • 2Add or remove entire groups of users at once.
  • 3Check "Email announcement" to notify users when added/removed. The email text is pre-populated but editable.
  • 4Under Add Users with CSV, enter comma-separated email addresses to bulk-invite. Users not registered with OPRA will receive an invite email and are automatically added once they register.
Step 4 — Algorithms, Visibility & UI
  • 1Algorithms — Select which algorithms compute and display results.
  • 2Visibility — Control whether voters can see other responses and whether they remain anonymous.
  • 3Accessibility — Set who can participate and whether self sign-up is allowed.
  • 4UI for preferences — Choose which voting UI(s) voters can use.
Poll Settings
  • Access from the poll detail page to modify selected configurations after creation.
  • Preference Management — View and delete submitted preferences (based on visibility settings).
  • Email — Opt-in to notify voters when the poll starts and stops. Email text is pre-populated but editable.
  • Duplicate / Delete — Duplicate the poll at its current state, or permanently delete it.
Voting UIs
UIHow it works
Two ColumnsDrag options from the right column to the left to set your preference order.
One ColumnRank all options in a single sortable list.
SlidersUse sliders to assign numeric preference scores.
StarsRate each option with a star rating.
Yes / NoApprove or reject each option.
Poll Results

Results are calculated based on the algorithms selected during poll creation and displayed on the Results page once the poll ends or is stopped.
